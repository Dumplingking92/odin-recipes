Charsiu Bao Recipe

Description

Pork bao made with sweet and salty and umami filling with a tender and sweet dough

Ingredients

  • Low gluten flour
  • Wheat starch
  • Water
  • Pork
  • Char siu sauce
  • Sauted onions
  • Smelly powder
  • Kan Sui
  • seasonings

Steps

  1. Combine flour, wheat starch,smelly powder and kansui together with water mix until dough forms
  2. mix the rest of the ingredients into a sticky mixture
  3. pleat the fucking dumpling like a boss
