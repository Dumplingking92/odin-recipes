Hargau Recipe

Description

Chinese dumpling recipe made with wheat starch and potato starch with prawns and bambu as filling

Ingredients

  • wheat starch
  • potato starch
  • boiling water
  • shrimp
  • bambu
  • sesame oil
  • pork fat
  • seasonings

Method

  1. Combine both starchs with hot water
  2. mix the rest of the ingredients into a sticky mixture
  3. pleat the fucking dumpling like a boss
